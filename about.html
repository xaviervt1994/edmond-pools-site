About Edmond Pools

Edmond Pools has proudly served the Edmond, OK community with quality pool maintenance, repair, and renovation services for over a decade. Our mission is to deliver reliable, affordable, and expert pool care so families and businesses can enjoy clean, safe, and beautiful pools all year long.

Our Story

What started as a small one-man operation has grown into a trusted local team known for attention to detail, honest work, and long-term customer relationships. We’ve built pools, rebuilt trust, and earned referrals by doing things the right way — every time.

Our team is made up of certified pool professionals, customer service experts, and technicians who genuinely care. We're not just pool guys — we're people who take pride in being part of the community and helping homeowners enjoy their backyard oasis.
